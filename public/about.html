Our Story

We are educators in Singapore, and we spend countless hours with students, parents, and fellow educators to help everyone reach their full potential. We've noticed that families and educators often struggle to find reliable, engaging educational content that actually works.

We asked ourselves: "What if we could create educational resources that feel as engaging as they are effective?" That's when AskCher was born - a platform where innovative teaching strategies meet practical parenting advice and student-focused study guides.

We're turning education into an accessible, enjoyable, and empowering experience. Today, thousands of families and educators use AskCher to enhance their learning journeys.
