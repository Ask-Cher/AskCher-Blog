Our Story

We are both students and educators in Singapore, and we spend countless hours with students, parents, and fellow educators to help everyone reach their full potential. The reality of the Singapore education system is one where both students and parents feel the stress. In some cases, parents feel it more than students. Having worked closely with parents and students ranging from 9 to 19 years old, we've seen it all - the different worries, struggles and issues that parents and students run into.

The education system is not perfect, but there are ways we can emerge from it victorious. That's our goal here at AskCher - to empower parents and students in the Singapore education system.

We're not here to sell you tuition lessons, we're here to impart tried and tested approaches to the different aspects of education - from study techniques to building discipline and a lifelong passion for learning.
